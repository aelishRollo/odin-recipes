Modular Salad

Description

Possibly the most versatile of all potential recipes in the multiverse, the one, the only, SALAD. Salad consistents of 4 elements: greens, protien, dressing, and the extras. These can all very to an extreme degree, so the possibilites are essentially endless. We'll go more into depth on these later on.

Ingredients

  • greens
  • protien
  • dressing
  • extras

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. In terms of greens, the amount of options is immense. Good options are spinach, kale, or iceburg lettuce.
  3. Meats are always a good protien, as can be tofu, hummus, or beans.
  4. I'm partial to italian, ceasar, and especially balsamic viniagrette.
  5. I'm fond of bacon bits, cheese (feta, bleu, cheddar, provalone),
