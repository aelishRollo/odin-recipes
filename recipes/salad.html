Modular Salad

Description

Possibly the most versatile of all potential recipes in the multiverse, the one, the only, SALAD. Salad consistents of 4 elements: greens, protien, dressing, and the extras. These can all very to an extreme degree, so the possibilites are essentially endless. We'll go more into depth on these lat er on.

Ingredients

  • greens
  • protien
  • dressing
  • extras

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400F
  2. Add your chicken wings to the pan, coated in butter/olive oil/avacado oil, mixed with spices of your choosing. I like salt and pepper, garlic, onion, lemon, basil. These are tasty in any combination.
  3. Add your veggies to the oven in their own pan, with their own seasonings.
  4. After 20 to 25 minutes, remove your veggie pan from the oven. This timing works well for broccoli and brussels sprouts.
  5. After 40 minutes, remove your pan from the oven, allow it to cool off, and feast like the raiders of yore. You can also leave the wings in for another 15 minutes if you like them extra crispy.
